Prefer the classic macOS shortcuts?

You can use ⌘⇧3, ⌘⇧4 and ⌘⇧5 with Screen for me once macOS releases them.

Step 1 — Free the shortcuts

In System Settings, open Keyboard → Keyboard Shortcuts… → Screenshots and untick the ⌘⇧3, ⌘⇧4 and ⌘⇧5 items.
